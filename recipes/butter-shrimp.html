Garlic Butter Shrimp

Delicious, satisfying, and oh-so-comforting garlic butter shrimp. Serve over rice or pasta.

Ingridents

½ cup butter 2 pounds frozen medium shrimp 2 tablespoons garlic powder, or more to taste ¼ cup water 2 tablespoons cornstarch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Melt butter in a large skillet or wok over medium-low heat. Add shrimp; cook and stir until shrimp turn pink, about 5 minutes. Mix in garlic powder. Whisk water and cornstarch together in a bowl to make a slurry. Pour into the skillet and stir until butter sauce thickens, 3 to 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
